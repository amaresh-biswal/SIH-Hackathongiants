Package 1: Mumbai

Day 1: Arrive in Mumbai and check-in to your hotel. In the evening, visit the [Gateway of India] and [Elephanta Island].

Day 2: Visit the [Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus], the [Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya], the [Haji Ali Dargah], and the [Siddhivinayak Temple].

Day 3: Take a day trip to [Lonavala] and [Khandala], where you can see the [Bhaja Caves], the [Lohagad Fort], the [Lion’s Point], the [Lonavala Lake], the [Rajmachi Fort], the [Duke’s Nose], and the [Bushi Dam].

Day 4: Explore the [Dharavi Slum], the [Bandra-Worli Sea Link], and some of the landmarks and celebrity homes in Bandra. Visit the Juhu Beach^ in the evening.

Day 5: Take a Bollywood tour and see how films are made and learn about the history and culture of Bollywood. Watch a Bollywood movie at one of the theatres in Mumbai.
