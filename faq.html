Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to Bharat Safari, the ultimate travel site for exploring the diverse and beautiful destinations of India. Here are some of the common questions that our customers ask us. If you have any other queries, please feel free to contact us.

  1. What is Bharat Safari?

    2. Bharat Safari is a travel site that offers customized and affordable packages for visiting various places in India. We aim to provide you with the best travel experience and help you discover the rich culture, heritage, and nature of India.

  2. How can I book a package with Bharat Safari?

    3. You can book a package with Bharat Safari by following these simple steps:

    • Visit our website and browse through our available packages. You can filter them by destination, duration, budget, and theme.
    • Select the package that suits your preferences and click on the "Book Now" button.
    • Fill in your personal details, travel dates, and payment information. You can also customize your package by adding or removing any services or activities.
    • Confirm your booking and receive a confirmation email with your itinerary and invoice.
  3. What are the benefits of booking with Bharat Safari?

    4. Booking with Bharat Safari has many benefits, such as:

    • You get to choose from a wide range of packages that cater to different tastes and interests.
    • You get to enjoy competitive prices and discounts on various services and activities.
    • You get to travel with expert guides and drivers who ensure your safety and comfort.
    • You get to access our 24/7 customer support and assistance in case of any issues or emergencies.
  4. What are the payment options available with Bharat Safari?

    5. We accept various payment options, such as:

    • Credit cards
    • Debit cards
    • Net banking
    • UPI
    • Wallets
    • Cash on delivery (for selected packages)

    We use secure and encrypted payment gateways to ensure your privacy and security.

  5. What is the cancellation policy of Bharat Safari?

    6. We understand that sometimes plans change and you may need to cancel your booking. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

    • If you cancel more than 30 days before your departure date, you will get a full refund minus a nominal service charge.
    • If you cancel between 15 to 30 days before your departure date, you will get a 50% refund minus a nominal service charge.
    • If you cancel less than 15 days before your departure date, you will not get any refund.

    Please note that some services or activities may have their own cancellation policies, which will be mentioned in your itinerary.
