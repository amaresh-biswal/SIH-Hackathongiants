Privacy Policy

Bharat Safari is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information when you use our website and services. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your data, and what choices you have regarding your data. Please read this policy carefully before using our website and services.

  1. What data do we collect?

    2. We collect two types of data from you:

    • Personal data: This is the data that can identify you as an individual, such as your name, email address, phone number, address, payment information, etc. We collect this data when you register on our website, book a package with us, contact us, or participate in any surveys or promotions.
    • Non-personal data: This is the data that cannot identify you as an individual, such as your browser type, device type, IP address, location, pages visited, time spent, etc. We collect this data using cookies and other tracking technologies when you access our website or services.
  2. How do we use your data?

    3. We use your data for various purposes, such as:

    • To provide you with the best travel experience and help you discover the diverse and beautiful destinations of India.
    • To process your booking and payment, and send you confirmation and invoice emails.
    • To communicate with you regarding your booking, travel arrangements, feedback, queries, or complaints.
    • To improve our website and services by analyzing your preferences, behavior, and feedback.
    • To personalize our website and services by showing you relevant content, offers, and advertisements.
    • To comply with any legal obligations or requests from authorities.
  3. How do we share your data?

    4. We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third parties. We only share your data with the following entities:

    • Our partners and suppliers: We share your data with our network of partners and suppliers who provide various travel services, such as accommodation, transportation, sightseeing, activities, etc., that are part of your package. We ensure that they follow our privacy policy and standards when handling your data.
    • Our service providers: We share your data with our service providers who perform certain functions on our behalf, such as payment processing, email marketing, web hosting, analytics, etc. We ensure that they have adequate security measures and confidentiality agreements when handling your data.
    • Our affiliates: We share your data with our affiliates who are part of the Bharat Safari group of companies. They may use your data to offer you other products or services that may interest you.
    • Other parties: We may share your data with other parties if we have your consent or if we are required by law or court order to do so.
  4. How do we protect your data?

    5. We take various technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction. Some of these measures are:

    • We use secure and encrypted payment gateways to ensure your privacy and security.
    • We use SSL certificates to encrypt the communication between your browser and our server.
    • We use firewalls and antivirus software to prevent any malicious attacks on our website and server.
    • We limit the access to your data to only those employees who need it for their work purposes.
    • We train our employees on the best practices of data protection and privacy.

    However, no method of transmission or storage of data is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data. You are also responsible for keeping your password and account details confidential and safe.

  5. What are your choices regarding your data?

    6. You have certain rights and choices regarding your data, such as:

    • You can access, update, correct, or delete your personal data by logging into your account on our website or contacting us at info@bharatsafari.com
    • You can opt-out of receiving any promotional emails or newsletters from us by clicking on the unsubscribe link in the email or contacting us at info@bharatsafari.com
    • You can disable or delete any cookies or other tracking technologies that we use on our website by changing your browser settings or using third-party tools.
    • You can contact us at info@bharatsafari.com if you have any questions, complaints, or requests regarding your data or this privacy policy.
